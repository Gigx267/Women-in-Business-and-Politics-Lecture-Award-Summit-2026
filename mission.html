Our Directive

Driving Structural Leadership Transformation

Our mission is to accelerate the rise of women in decision-making positions across business and governance, creating a more equitable and prosperous Africa.

2026 Roadmap

Strategic Goals for This Year

The 2026 edition is designed to be our most impactful yet.

Each goal is measurable and tied to concrete outcomes for attendees, award recipients, and the broader ecosystem.

01
Recognize 50+ Outstanding Women

Honor a diverse cohort of trailblazers across all 12 award categories, with expanded focus on emerging leaders under 40.

02
Facilitate 200+ Strategic Connections

Through structured networking sessions, B2B matchmaking, and mentorship pairings that outlast the event.

03
Produce a Policy Communiqué

Deliver actionable recommendations to government and private sector leaders on advancing women's leadership.

04
Launch a Year-Round Mentorship Program

Move beyond a one-day summit by establishing a structured mentorship initiative connecting established leaders with emerging talents.

05
Achieve 40% First-Time Attendee Participation

Expand our reach by ensuring significant representation of new voices, particularly from underserved regions and sectors.
