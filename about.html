About the Summit

The Women in Business and Politics Lecture & Award Summit is a premium annual gathering that celebrates, connects, and empowers Nigeria's most influential female leaders across the corporate, political, and social sectors.

Our Story

A Movement That Started in 2020

The Women in Business and Politics Summit was born from a simple but powerful idea: to create a premier platform where Nigeria's most accomplished women leaders could connect, share insights, and drive meaningful change.

What began as a modest gathering of 50 women has grown into the country's most prestigious forum for female leadership excellence, attracting ministers, CEOs, diplomats, and changemakers from across Africa.

Our Journey

Milestones of Impact

From a bold vision to a nationwide movement—track our growth and impact.

2020
Inaugural Summit

First edition hosted with 50 women leaders, introducing the concept of a dedicated platform for female excellence in business and politics.

2022
Expansion to 12 Award Categories

Introduced a comprehensive award structure recognizing excellence across diverse sectors including public service, technology, and healthcare.

2024
Regional Reach & Partnerships

Expanded to include delegates from West Africa, securing partnerships with major corporate organizations and international development agencies.

2026
The Paradigm Edition

Our most ambitious summit yet—celebrating excellence, leadership, and lasting impact with over 500 expected attendees and 50 award recipients.
